When you click Design and choose a new Theme, the pictures, charts, and SmartArt graphics change to match your new theme. To make your document look professionally produced, Word provides header, footer, cover page, and text box designs that complement each other. When you click Online Video, you can paste in the embed code for the video you want to add. When you work on a table, click where you want to add a row or a column, and then click the plus sign. To change the way a picture fits in your document, click it and a button for layout options appears next to it.
